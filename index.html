Our Inspiration

Late Shri Om Prakash Chalka, a visionary educationist best known for his impeccable professional integrity, scientific temperament and dynamism, and implementation of progressive and innovative ideas at grass root level, who worked relentlessly to create a self-reliant rural society and by working within the system, endeavored to counter its regressive tendencies.

The Foundation has been established to carry forward the cherished dream of Late Shri O.P. Chalka, as a visionary educationist and Philanthropist, to promote education, develop social awareness and traditional environment conservation.
